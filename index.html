About#

Community for players who treat games as structured problem‑solving.

We are all about multi‑game tournaments and game nights. Our philosophy is that strategy games are more than just games. They are a way to sharpen one's mind, especially if the games played are diverse and complex.

Strategic Duelists believe that putting in thousands of hours into a particular game doesn't correlate with improving decision-making skills beyond said game. However, putting in thousands of hours into a variety of strategy games does.

Cash games and tournaments are allowed to spice things up.
